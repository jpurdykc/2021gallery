Welcome to the 2020-21 Keuka College Annual Student Showcase


What a year to flex our creative muscles! While we traditionally hold this Student Exhibit on campus and with amazing music, food and discussions, this year we have had to deliver something we’ve never done before -- a VIRTUAL exhibition.


The benefit? You get to see our work on your schedule and take your time looking at each piece without us anxiously watching for your reactions. What we hope is that you don’t keep your reaction to yourselves -- it is virtual but your thoughts, ideas and feedback are more critically important to us than ever.


This collection is a celebration of our best student work -- and the classes the work emerged from include:

Drawing

Painting

Foundational Design

Graphic Design

Video Production

Photography

Mixed Media

Ceramic Sculpture

Advanced Studies


We are VERY proud to share this with you and welcome your feedback and thoughts. Your support of our work is incredibly important and if you’re so inclined to purchase our work or give us feedback directly please contact our professor Melissa Newcomb by email at mnewcomb@keuka.edu.


Thank you for your support!
